about booksmart

BOOKSMART is an unfiltered coming of age comedy for the new generation. Following two best friends, Molly (BEANIE FELDSTEIN) and Amy (KAITLYN DEVER) on a wild adventure. The day before their graduation the best friends try all the parties and crazy nights they have missed all in one night.
